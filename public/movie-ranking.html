Alex's Top 3 Movies!

Seriously, you have to give these a watch...

Hot Fuzz

A Tim Wright movie about cops and action movie tropes, this fun action flick stars Nock Frost and Simon Pegg. The humor is very toungue in cheek and this is noe one to miss!


A Few Good Men

A fantastic military legal drama about the defense of two Marines accused or murder. This one stars the always fun to watch Tom Cruise!


The Shawshank Redemption

Widely considered one of the best films ever made, we follow the wrongfully accused and convicted banker, Andy Dufresne, as he copes with his new life behind bars.
